# Contoso Corporation - Employee Benefits Overview

## Benefits Eligibility

Full-time employees (30+ hours/week) are eligible for benefits on the **1st of the month**
following their hire date. Part-time employees (20-29 hours/week) are eligible for
limited benefits.

## Health Insurance

### Medical Plans

Contoso offers three medical plan options through Blue Cross Blue Shield:

| Plan | Monthly Premium (Employee) | Monthly Premium (Family) | Deductible | Out-of-Pocket Max |
|------|---------------------------|-------------------------|------------|-------------------|
| PPO Basic | $150 | $450 | $1,500 | $6,000 |
| PPO Plus | $250 | $650 | $750 | $4,000 |
| HDHP + HSA | $100 | $300 | $2,800 | $7,000 |

### Dental Insurance

- **Basic Plan**: $25/month (employee), $75/month (family)
  - Preventive care: 100% covered
  - Basic procedures: 80% covered
  - Major procedures: 50% covered

- **Premium Plan**: $45/month (employee), $125/month (family)
  - Preventive care: 100% covered
  - Basic procedures: 90% covered
  - Major procedures: 70% covered
  - Orthodontics: 50% covered (lifetime max $2,500)

### Vision Insurance

- **Standard Plan**: $10/month (employee), $25/month (family)
  - Annual eye exam: $10 copay
  - Frames allowance: $150 every 2 years
  - Contact lens allowance: $150/year

## Retirement Benefits

### 401(k) Plan

- **Eligibility**: Immediate upon hire
- **Company Match**: 100% match on first 4% of salary, 50% match on next 2%
- **Vesting Schedule**:
  - Year 1: 33% vested
  - Year 2: 66% vested
  - Year 3: 100% vested
- **Investment Options**: 25+ fund options including target-date funds
- **Provider**: Fidelity Investments

### Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)

- Purchase Contoso stock at **15% discount**
- Contribution limit: 10% of salary (up to $25,000/year)
- Purchase periods: Semi-annual (June 30 and December 31)

## Life & Disability Insurance

### Life Insurance

- **Basic Life**: 1x annual salary (company paid, up to $500,000)
- **Supplemental Life**: Up to 5x annual salary (employee paid)
- **Spouse/Dependent Life**: Available for purchase

### Disability Insurance

- **Short-Term Disability**: 60% of salary for up to 26 weeks (company paid)
- **Long-Term Disability**: 60% of salary after 26 weeks (company paid)

## Additional Benefits

### Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA)

- **Healthcare FSA**: Up to $3,050/year for medical expenses
- **Dependent Care FSA**: Up to $5,000/year for childcare expenses

### Health Savings Account (HSA)

Available with HDHP plan only:

- **Individual**: Up to $4,150/year
- **Family**: Up to $8,300/year
- **Catch-up (55+)**: Additional $1,000/year
- Company contributes **$750 (individual)** or **$1,500 (family)** annually

### Wellness Programs

- **Gym Reimbursement**: Up to $50/month for fitness memberships
- **Wellness Challenges**: Quarterly programs with prizes
- **Mental Health**: Free access to Calm app premium
- **Employee Assistance Program (EAP)**: 6 free counseling sessions/year

### Professional Development

- **Tuition Reimbursement**: Up to $5,250/year for approved programs
- **Conference Attendance**: Budget varies by department
- **LinkedIn Learning**: Free access for all employees
- **Internal Training**: Leadership development programs available

### Parental Leave

- **Birth Parent**: 16 weeks paid leave
- **Non-Birth Parent**: 8 weeks paid leave
- **Adoption/Foster**: 8 weeks paid leave
- **Gradual Return**: Option for part-time schedule for 4 weeks after leave

### Other Perks

- **Commuter Benefits**: Pre-tax transit/parking up to IRS limits
- **Cell Phone Stipend**: $75/month for eligible roles
- **Home Office Setup**: $500 one-time allowance for remote workers
- **Employee Discounts**: Access to corporate discount programs

## Open Enrollment

Open enrollment occurs annually in **November** for coverage effective January 1.
Life events (marriage, birth, etc.) allow for mid-year changes within 30 days.
