# Contoso Corporation - Company Information

## About Contoso

Contoso Corporation is a multinational technology company headquartered in
Redmond, Washington. Founded in 1995, Contoso has grown to over 12,000 employees
across 15 countries.

## Company Values

1. **Innovation First** - We encourage creative solutions and continuous improvement
2. **People Matter** - Our employees are our greatest asset
3. **Integrity Always** - We do the right thing, even when no one is watching
4. **Customer Focus** - Every decision considers our customers' needs
