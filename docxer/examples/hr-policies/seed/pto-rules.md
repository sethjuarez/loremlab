# Contoso Corporation - PTO Policy Details

## Paid Time Off (PTO) Overview

Contoso provides a comprehensive Paid Time Off program that combines vacation,
personal days, and sick leave into a single flexible PTO bank.

## PTO Accrual Rates

PTO accrues based on years of service:

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Accrual | Monthly Accrual |
|------------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| 0-2 years | 15 days (120 hours) | 10 hours |
| 3-5 years | 20 days (160 hours) | 13.33 hours |
| 6-10 years | 25 days (200 hours) | 16.67 hours |
| 11+ years | 30 days (240 hours) | 20 hours |

## PTO Carryover Policy

- Employees may carry over up to **5 days (40 hours)** of unused PTO to the next calendar year
- Carryover PTO must be used by **March 31** of the following year
- PTO in excess of carryover limits will be forfeited on December 31
- Exception requests may be submitted to HR for special circumstances

## Requesting Time Off

### Process

1. Submit request through **Workday** at least 2 weeks in advance for planned PTO
2. Manager approval required for requests of 3+ consecutive days
3. Requests of less than 3 days require manager notification (no approval needed)
4. Emergency/sick time can be reported same-day through Workday

### Blackout Periods

The following periods have restricted PTO approval due to business needs:

- **Year-end close**: December 26-31 (Finance department)
- **Product launches**: As announced (Engineering teams)
- **Quarter-end**: Last 5 business days of each quarter (Sales teams)

## Sick Leave

While PTO is a single bank, employees experiencing illness should:

- Notify their manager by **9:00 AM** on the day of absence
- Use the "Sick" category when logging time in Workday
- Provide a doctor's note for absences exceeding **3 consecutive days**

## Holidays

Contoso observes the following **11 paid holidays**:

1. New Year's Day (January 1)
2. Martin Luther King Jr. Day (3rd Monday in January)
3. Presidents' Day (3rd Monday in February)
4. Memorial Day (Last Monday in May)
5. Independence Day (July 4)
6. Labor Day (1st Monday in September)
7. Thanksgiving Day (4th Thursday in November)
8. Day After Thanksgiving (4th Friday in November)
9. Christmas Eve (December 24)
10. Christmas Day (December 25)
11. Personal Floating Holiday (employee choice)

### Floating Holiday

- Each employee receives **1 floating holiday** per calendar year
- Must be used within the calendar year (no carryover)
- Request through Workday like regular PTO
- Can be used for religious observances, birthdays, or any personal reason

## PTO Payout

- Upon voluntary resignation with 2+ weeks notice: 100% of accrued PTO paid out
- Upon voluntary resignation with less than 2 weeks notice: 50% of accrued PTO paid out
- Upon involuntary termination: 100% of accrued PTO paid out
- PTO payout included in final paycheck
